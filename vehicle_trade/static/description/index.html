Vehicle Trade Features

Advanced Dashboard

The dashboard displays vehicle counts, available and sold vehicles, discussions, payment statuses, and monthly vendor bills.

Vehicle Sellers

'Sold By' (Dealer or Private), contact information provided, encompassing phone number and email for communication purposes.

Vehicle Images

Images depict sold vehicle's features, condition, appearance, providing valuable insights for potential buyers.

Vehicle Specifications

Accurate vehicle specifications inform buyers about features, playing a vital role in the selling process's success.

Vehicle History

Including vehicle's history details when selling provides crucial information to potential buyers, enhancing transparency and trust.

Documents

Access and download vehicle selling documents at your convenience, ensuring ease and flexibility in the process.

Expert Inspections

Expert inspection assures vehicle quality, assessing mechanical components, body integrity, and performance pre-sale.

Vehicles

Trade workflow: new, available, discussion, sold. Vehicle details: brand, model, fuel type, condition, year, license, odometer, value.

Vehicle Buyers

'Sold to' (Dealer, Private, or Auction), provided with contact information including phone number and email.

Previous Service History

A vehicle's service history reassures buyers of its maintenance, enhancing its appeal and value during sale.

Vehicle Conditions

Vehicle part condition is vital for selling, impacting reliability, functionality, and potential maintenance costs for buyers.

Insurance History

When selling a vehicle, providing insurance history details, including current, expired policies, coverage duration, is crucial.

Reports

The vehicle sample report offers comprehensive details crucial for facilitating the vehicle selling process effectively.

Sale & Purchase

Throughout the vehicle selling process, vendor bills, down payment, final invoice, vehicle price, taxes, and fees are documented.
